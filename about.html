The Science

What is Vastu Shastra

The ancient Indian science of architecture and spatial design — a 5,000-year-old body of knowledge governing the design, layout and geometry of living spaces.

"Vastu is a Sanskrit word — Vaas: to live, Tu: you. A place where you live or dwell. Shastra means a text which contains knowledge or instructions."

Panchabhutas

Balancing the five elements — Earth, Water, Fire, Air and Space — within your environment.

Directional Energy

Each of the eight directions carries specific cosmic energies governing health, wealth and relationships.

Sacred Geometry

Precise proportion and geometry of spaces creates resonance with universal harmony.

Cosmic Alignment

Synchronizing your living space with the movement of the sun, moon and planetary influences.
